The Approach

Four models. One pipeline. Fundable outcomes.

Humanitai combines four proven models into a single system: community health workers collect ground truth, MiroFish predicts crisis, Social Impact Bonds fund the work, SROI proves the return.

Model 1
👥

CHW Intelligence

Trained community liaisons in UK neighbourhoods collecting real-time ground truth: needs assessments, photo evidence, location-stamped reports.

Model 2
🧠

MiroFish Swarm AI

Seven dimensions analysed across 12+ data sources. Five agent clusters debate from different perspectives to predict where intervention prevents crisis.

Model 3
💰

Social Impact Bonds

Investors fund interventions upfront. Government pays back only if outcomes are achieved. $745M capital deployed globally across 276 SIBs.

Model 4
📊

SROI Measurement

UK-government-recognised framework. Every pound tracked. Every outcome verified. "For every £1 invested, £X social value returned."

Straight Answers

What people ask before they back us.

What exactly does Humanitai do?
We deploy trained CHW intelligence teams into UK neighbourhoods with the worst verified outcomes. They collect real ground-truth data — needs assessments, intake forms, photo evidence — which feeds our MiroFish swarm AI to predict where crisis will hit next. No dashboards. Real people, on the street.
Why not just give the data to the council?
Because the data already exists and still nothing changes. Humanitai adds the measurement + outcome layer that Social Impact Bonds require. Funders pay only when verified outcomes are achieved.
Is this another charity asking for donations?
No. We are a Community Interest Company — asset-locked, dividend-capped. Built to be funded through Social Impact Bonds: investors fund interventions, government repays only on proven outcomes.
How do you prove the outcomes are real?
SROI — the UK-government-recognised 8-principle framework from Social Value UK. Every pound tracked. Every outcome verified onsite.
Where do you operate?
Currently mapping 18 UK regions — from Glasgow to Newham, Bristol to Blackpool. See the live deployment map above.
