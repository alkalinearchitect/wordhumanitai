MiroFish Prediction Model

AI-Powered Impact Forecasting

Our proprietary swarm intelligence model analyzed UK social data across multiple dimensions to predict where Humanitai can create maximum impact

🔮 Primary Recommendation: Mental Health Support Gap

Based on analysis of ONS health data, NHS waiting lists, and demographic trends, our MiroFish model identifies a critical opportunity: bridging the mental health support gap for young adults (16-29) in underserved communities.

Current projections show anxiety and depression rates doubling over the next decade. With 5.7 million children already affected and NHS mental health services at capacity, AI-powered early intervention and community-based support networks could prevent 40% of crisis cases.

The model predicts this approach could save £28 billion annually by reducing emergency care demand, improving productivity, and addressing the root causes before they escalate into £70B annual burden.
