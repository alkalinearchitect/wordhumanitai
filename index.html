The architecture

Four layers. One pipeline.

OSINT data gathering → multi-agent swarm prediction → CHW ground truth → SROI-verified outcomes.

🔍Layer 1

OSINT Data

12+ public datasets from ONS, NHS, Trussell Trust, JRF, gov.uk — gathered via API, browser automation, and web intelligence.

🧠Layer 2

Human Architect

MiroFish multi-agent swarm (69K⭐). Neo4j knowledge graph, 5 agent clusters, OASIS social simulation. Confidence-scored forecasts.

👥Layer 3

CHW Teams

Community health workers deployed to high-risk neighbourhoods verify predictions with real needs assessments and photo evidence.

📊Layer 4

SIB + SROI

Social Impact Bonds fund interventions. SROI framework (UK Govt-recognised) verifies every pound. Investors paid on outcomes.

Straight answers

What people ask before they back us.

What exactly does Humanitai do?
We deploy trained CHW intelligence teams into UK neighbourhoods with the worst verified outcomes. They collect real ground-truth data — needs assessments, intake forms, photo evidence — which feeds our Human Architect Technology (OSINT + MiroFish multi-agent AI) to predict where crisis will hit next.
Why not just give the data to the council?
Because the data already exists and still nothing changes. Humanitai adds the measurement + outcome layer that Social Impact Bonds require.
Is this another charity?
No. We are a Community Interest Company — asset-locked, dividend-capped. Funded through Social Impact Bonds.
How do you prove outcomes?
SROI — the UK-government-recognised 8-principle framework. Every pound tracked. Every outcome verified.
Where do you operate?
18 UK regions — from Glasgow to Newham, Bristol to Blackpool. See the intelligence dashboard above.
