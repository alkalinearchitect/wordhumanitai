Model 1 — Ground Truth

Community Health Worker Intelligence

Trained community liaisons in UK neighbourhoods collect real-time needs assessments, photo evidence, and location-stamped reports. 1.3M+ CHWs worldwide proven to cut infant mortality 42% (Brazil) and maternal mortality 73% (Iran).

Model 2 — Prediction

MiroFish Swarm AI

Seven dimensions across 12+ data sources. Five agent clusters debate from different perspectives to predict where intervention prevents crisis. Confidence-scored forecasts using the same pattern RAND Corporation identified.

Model 3 — Funding

Social Impact Bonds

Investors fund interventions upfront. Government pays back only if outcomes are achieved. $745M deployed globally across 276 SIBs. UK pioneered this model at Peterborough Prison in 2010.

Model 4 — Proof

SROI Outcome Measurement

UK-government-recognised 8-principle framework. Social outcomes converted to monetary value. "For every £1 invested, £X social value returned." Every pound tracked. Every outcome verified onsite.

Straight Answers

What people ask before they back us.

What exactly does Humanitai do?
We deploy trained CHW intelligence teams into UK neighbourhoods with the worst verified outcomes. They collect real ground-truth data — needs assessments, intake forms, photo evidence — which feeds our MiroFish swarm AI to predict where crisis will hit next.
Why not just give the data to the council?
Because the data already exists and still nothing changes. Humanitai adds the measurement + outcome layer that Social Impact Bonds require. Funders pay only when verified outcomes are achieved.
Is this another charity asking for donations?
No. We are a Community Interest Company — asset-locked, dividend-capped. Built to be funded through Social Impact Bonds: investors fund interventions, government repays only on proven outcomes.
How do you prove the outcomes are real?
SROI — the UK-government-recognised 8-principle framework from Social Value UK. Every pound tracked. Every outcome verified onsite.
Where do you operate?
Currently mapping 18 UK regions — from Glasgow to Newham, Bristol to Blackpool. See the live deployment map above.
