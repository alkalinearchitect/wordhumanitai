The architecture

Four layers. One pipeline.

OSINT data gathering → multi-agent swarm prediction → CHW ground truth → SROI-verified outcomes.

🔍Layer 1

OSINT Data

12+ public datasets from ONS, NHS, Trussell Trust, JRF, gov.uk — gathered via API, browser automation, and web intelligence.

🧠Layer 2

Human Architect

MiroFish multi-agent swarm (69K⭐). Neo4j knowledge graph, 5 agent clusters, OASIS social simulation. Confidence-scored forecasts across 7 dimensions.

👥Layer 3

CHW Teams

Community health workers in high-risk neighbourhoods verify predictions with real needs assessments, photo evidence, and location-stamped reports.

📊Layer 4

SIB + SROI

Social Impact Bonds fund interventions. UK Govt-recognised SROI framework verifies every pound. Investors paid only when outcomes are achieved.

Straight answers

What people ask before they back us.

What exactly does Humanitai do?
We deploy trained CHW intelligence teams into UK neighbourhoods with the worst verified outcomes. They collect real ground-truth data — needs assessments, intake forms, photo evidence — which feeds our Human Architect Technology (OSINT + MiroFish multi-agent AI) to predict where crisis will hit next.
Why not just give the data to the council?
Because the data already exists and still nothing changes. Humanitai adds the measurement + outcome layer that Social Impact Bonds require. Funders pay only when verified outcomes are achieved.
Is this another charity asking for donations?
No. We are a Community Interest Company — asset-locked, dividend-capped. Built to be funded through Social Impact Bonds: investors fund interventions, government repays only on proven outcomes.
How do you prove the outcomes are real?
SROI — the UK-government-recognised 8-principle framework from Social Value UK. Every pound tracked. Every outcome verified.
Where do you operate?
Currently mapping 18 UK regions — from Glasgow to Newham, Bristol to Blackpool. See the intelligence dashboard above.
