Straight answers

What people ask before they back us.

What exactly does Humanitai do?
We deploy trained CHW intelligence teams into UK neighbourhoods with the worst verified outcomes. They collect real ground-truth data — needs assessments, intake forms, photo evidence — which feeds our Human Architect Technology (OSINT + AI) to predict where crisis will hit next.
Why not just give the data to the council?
Because the data already exists and still nothing changes. Humanitai adds the measurement + outcome layer that Social Impact Bonds require. Funders pay only when verified outcomes are achieved.
Is this another charity asking for donations?
No. We are a Community Interest Company — asset-locked, dividend-capped. Built to be funded through Social Impact Bonds: investors fund interventions, government repays only on proven outcomes.
How do you prove the outcomes are real?
SROI — the UK-government-recognised 8-principle framework from Social Value UK. Every pound tracked. Every outcome verified.
Where do you operate?
Currently mapping 18 UK regions — from Glasgow to Newham, Bristol to Blackpool. See the live deployment map above.
