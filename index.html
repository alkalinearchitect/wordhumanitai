Straight answers

What people ask before they back us.

What exactly does HumanitAI do?
We deploy trained community liaisons into UK neighbourhoods with the worst verified outcomes. They collect real ground-truth data, which feeds our forecasting model to predict where crisis will hit next — then act while the window is open. No dashboards for their own sake. Real people, on the street.
Why not just hand the data to the council?
Because the data already exists and still nothing changes. HumanitAI adds the measurement and outcome layer that Social Impact Bonds require. Funders pay only when verified outcomes are achieved.
Is this another charity asking for donations?
No. We are a Community Interest Company — asset-locked and dividend-capped. We are built to be funded through Social Impact Bonds: investors fund interventions, government repays only on proven outcomes.
How do you prove the outcomes are real?
Through SROI — the UK-government-recognised framework from Social Value UK. Every pound tracked, every outcome verified on-site before repayment is triggered.
Where do you operate?
We currently forecast 18 UK regions — from Glasgow to Newham, Bristol to Blackpool. Open the intelligence panel above to see the ranked list and the on-the-ground picture for each.
